2025 clinical trial highlights

A multi-centre trial spanning eight European fertility clinics, published in early 2025, tracked 1,240 vitrified blastocysts transported by specialist courier over distances of 400–4,800 km. The primary endpoint was intact zona pellucida and confirmed resumption of cell division within six hours of warming at the destination clinic. Results across all transport legs showed a 97.4% intact survival rate — statistically indistinguishable from in-clinic control transfers.
