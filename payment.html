Payments

Payment Methods

We accept a range of secure payment options. Payment is due after your shipment is confirmed and all documentation is in order.

Credit & Debit Card

Pay securely online by card. We accept all major card networks.

VISA Mastercard Amex

You will receive a secure payment link by email once your shipment is confirmed. No card details are stored by CryoLine.

Bank Wire Transfer

International bank transfer (SWIFT/SEPA) accepted in EUR and USD.

Bank details are provided on your invoice after booking confirmation. Wire transfers typically clear within 1–3 business days. Please send your transfer confirmation to us so we can begin permit preparation in parallel.

Corporate Invoice

Clinics and fertility centres can request invoiced billing on net-30 terms after an initial order.

Contact us to discuss a partner account. We work with clinics across Europe and the Caucasus on recurring shipment programmes with consolidated invoicing.

Process

When do I pay?

1
Request & confirm

Submit your delivery request. We confirm feasibility, paperwork requirements, and the exact price — usually the same day.

2
Invoice issued

Once confirmed, you receive a formal invoice with payment instructions. No payment is required before this point.

3
Payment clears

We begin permit preparation and container charging as soon as payment is confirmed. For wire transfers, sending your receipt speeds this up.

4
Shipment dispatched

Courier collects the specimen. From here, live tracking and your coordinator keep you informed until delivery.

Questions about payment?

Contact us directly — we'll walk you through the process and answer any questions before you commit to anything.
