Equipment

Container capacity

We use validated vapor-phase liquid nitrogen dry shippers in two sizes. Container choice depends on specimen count and type.

Small 1.5 L
  • Up to 3 cryo canes + 3 free wires
  • Or 1 goblet (7 straws)
  • Suitable for small sperm or oocyte batches
Large 3.5 L
  • Up to 13 cryo canes + 5 vials
  • Or 2 goblets (7 straws each)
  • Suitable for larger embryo or combined cohorts
