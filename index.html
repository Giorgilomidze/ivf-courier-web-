Safety & compliance

The technical detail behind a safe move

What actually keeps a specimen viable across a border isn't luck — it's validated equipment, documentation and dangerous-goods discipline.

Equipment

Vapor-phase dry shippers

Nitrogen is absorbed into the container walls, so there's no free liquid to leak — safe for air travel and stable for days.

  • Holds −196°C for the full transit window
  • Pre-conditioned and validated before every trip
  • Independent data logger inside each container
Documentation

Paperwork that clears customs

Missing consent or permits is the most common reason a specimen gets stuck. We prepare it up front, not at the border.

  • Import/export permits per jurisdiction
  • Patient consent & ownership records
  • Clinic-to-clinic transfer agreements
Transport

IATA dangerous-goods handling

Cryogenic shipments are regulated cargo. Our couriers are certified and our declarations are airline-ready.

  • IATA DGR-compliant declarations
  • Hand-carry option for high-value cohorts
  • Contingency routing if a flight is disrupted
